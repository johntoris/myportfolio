Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2025

Welcome to My website johntoris.com, the official website of John Toris, a professional journalist, mentor, and content creator. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting any personal information you share through this site. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit or interact with this website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from visitors:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, and any other details you provide when submitting a contact form, subscribing to a newsletter, or downloading the CV.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, and referring websites—collected via standard analytics tools like Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries or messages submitted via the contact form.
  • To personalize your experience on the site.
  • To improve website content and performance through analytics.
  • To send occasional updates or newsletters (only if you opt-in).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share non-personal, aggregated data for analytics purposes. If legally required, we may disclose information to comply with applicable laws or respond to lawful government requests.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us analyze site usage, remember preferences, and deliver content more effectively. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no online transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

This website may include links to third-party platforms or services, such as social media profiles or embedded media. These third-party websites have separate and independent privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies before engaging with them.

7. Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe that a child has submitted personal information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal data, correct inaccuracies, or request deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the form on the Contact page.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out via the Contact page.
